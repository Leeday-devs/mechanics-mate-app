Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What vehicles are supported?

We support 31 UK and European car manufacturers including Vauxhall, Ford, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Volkswagen, Toyota, Honda, and many more. Our database includes models from 2000 onwards with comprehensive engine type and size options.

How does the AI work?

Car Mechanic is powered by Claude 3.5 Sonnet from Anthropic, one of the most advanced AI models available. It's trained on comprehensive automotive knowledge and provides vehicle-specific advice based on your exact make, model, and year.

Can I use it offline?

Yes! Car Mechanic is a Progressive Web App (PWA) with offline support. You can install it on any device and access previously loaded content even without an internet connection. New questions require an internet connection to get AI responses.

What happens if I run out of messages?

If you reach your monthly message limit, you'll need to either upgrade to a higher plan or wait until your quota resets at the start of the next billing cycle. The Workshop plan offers unlimited messages.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use Supabase authentication with JWT tokens, encrypted HTTPS connections, Helmet security headers, and CORS protection. Your conversations are stored securely and you can export or delete them at any time.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your dashboard. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
